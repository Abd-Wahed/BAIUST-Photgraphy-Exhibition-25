Capturing Moments, Creating Memories, Celebrating Art

Exhibition Segments

Three distinct showcases celebrating different forms of visual storytelling and creative expression

Photo Exhibition

Stunning photography from talented students showcasing various genres including portrait, landscape, street, and conceptual photography.

  • Digital & Film Photography
  • Multiple Categories
  • Professional Judging
  • Cash Prizes
Apply
  • Submit 1 photograph only.
  • Must be taken by the participant (DSLR/Digital/Smartphone).
  • Only basic edits allowed; heavy manipulation disqualifies.
  • High-resolution JPEG/PNG format.
  • Theme announced during registration.
  • Rename file with your WhatsApp number before upload.
  • Duplicate submissions will be disqualified.

Arts & Craft Showcasing

Handcrafted masterpieces including paintings, sculptures, digital art, and traditional crafts representing diverse artistic expressions.

  • Traditional & Digital Art
  • Sculpture & 3D Works
  • Mixed Media
  • Interactive Displays
Apply
  • Submit 1 artwork/craft.
  • Only handmade items are accepted.
  • Sale via bidding system is allowed.
  • Club retains 25% of final selling price.
  • Framing must be done by participant.
  • Submit 10 days before the event.

Cinematography Showcasing

Short films, documentaries, and video projects demonstrating storytelling through motion pictures and innovative cinematographic techniques.

  • Short Films & Documentaries
  • Music Videos
  • Experimental Cinema
  • Screening Sessions
Apply
  • Video length: 1–3 minutes.
  • Solo or group (max 3 members).
  • Documentary, Short Film, Cinematic Story, or Campus-themed.
  • MP4 format, under 500 MB.
  • Use copyright-free or credited audio.
  • May be showcased for promotional purposes.

Official Rules & Regulations

Please review all terms and conditions carefully. Participation constitutes acceptance of these rules.

General Rules

  • Eligibility: Only students from colleges and universities may participate.
  • Registration: Complete registration before the deadline. Late entries not accepted.
  • Original Work: Must be original; plagiarism/copyright issues lead to disqualification.
  • Multiple Entries: Separate registrations required for each segment (payment may be combined).
  • Content: Obscene/offensive/culturally inappropriate content not accepted.
  • Usage Rights: Organizers may use submissions for promotion with credit.
  • Judgement: Judges’ decisions are final.
  • Presence: Must attend exhibition day in person.
  • Group Entries: Only for Cinematography (3–5 members).
  • Arts & Crafts Sales: Club retains 25% of selling price.
  • Model Photography: Not allowed.
  • Submission Window: Collection begins 10 days after registration opens.
  • Contact: Provide valid email and WhatsApp; communication via these.
  • Payment: Pay before filling form; correct transaction ID required.

Terms & Conditions

  • Grant BPC permission to display/use works for marketing.
  • Payments via bKash, Nagad, or Cash (cash only at campus booth).
  • Arts & Crafts works may be sold during the exhibition.
  • 25% of final Arts & Crafts sale goes to BPC fund.
